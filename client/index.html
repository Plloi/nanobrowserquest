Create a Nano Account


Nano is a feeless, instant, secure and green cryptocurrency, learn more. You will be earning a small payout for completing the game! You can create a free wallet by downloading a mobile wallet such as Natrium (iOS and Android) or Nalli (iOS and Android) or by using Nault (web-based).

Once your wallet is created, you'll get a your public address starting with "nano_", this address can be shared as a way to receive funds.

You'll also get a Nano seed / Nano mnemonic phrase. Create a backup and NEVER share your seed / mnemonic phrase with anyone, they are the keys to your funds. If they are lost you will lose access to your funds forever.
